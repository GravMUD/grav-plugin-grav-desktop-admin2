Got Grav Desktop installed? **We want to see it.**

Post screenshots of your setup — wallpapers (built-in, JavaBean, or custom), sticky notes, open windows, arcade high scores, the whole flight deck.

### How to share
1. Install Grav Desktop (see **Welcome** or **Announcements**)
2. Screenshot your desktop (Win+Shift+S or your OS tool)
3. Drop it here with your wallpaper preset + any notes on apps you use most
